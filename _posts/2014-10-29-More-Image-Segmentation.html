We used our [time derivative analysis](http://jallen30gt.github.io/MIC-Tungsten-Nanowires/2014/10/14/Time-Evolution.html) to aid in this decision. We also have an abberation in our images that affects our segmentation analysis as well. Using both of these pieces of information, we decided on running our segmentation analys on rows 200 - 700 and columns 400 - 700 on each of the images.
