--- layout: post title: Image Smoothing tagline: image smoothing author: jallen30gt date: 2014-11-06 16:00:00 latex: true --- # Smoothing Image Data Through Time Images have been smoothed over time using an averaging weighting function: $$A_{i}(smooth) = \frac{(0.5(A_{i+1})+A_{i}+0.5(A_{i-1}))}{2}$$
